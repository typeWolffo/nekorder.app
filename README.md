## Features

**Smart Auto-Zoom** - Nekorder tracks your cursor and generates smooth zoom-and-pan animations automatically. No manual keyframing required.

**Zoom Curve Editor** - A dedicated timeline lane lets you shape zoom levels as a visual curve. Drag control points, set zoom ranges, and place pan waypoints for precise camera control.

**Keystroke Display** - Keyboard shortcuts appear as clean, readable chips on the video. Toggle visibility, adjust size, and control display duration.

**Camera + Screen Sync** - Record your webcam alongside your screen with hardware-level synchronization. Zero audio drift.

**Cinematic Transitions** - Five easing modes: Linear, Ease In, Ease Out, Ease In-Out, and Spring physics.

**Cursor Enhancement** - Custom styles (arrow, circle, crosshair), adjustable size, click visualization, and smoothing.

**Visual Styling** - Gradient backgrounds, animated auroras, blur effects, adjustable padding, corner radius, and shadows.

**One-Click Export** - MP4 with HEVC encoding, up to 4K at 60fps.

**Native macOS** - Built on ScreenCaptureKit and AVFoundation. No Electron. No browser wrapper eating your RAM.
